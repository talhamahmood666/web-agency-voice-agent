# Frequently Asked Questions

These are the most common questions prospects ask on cold calls. Answer concisely — 2-3 sentences max — and pivot back to booking the consultation when appropriate.

---

## "What exactly do you do?"

We build professional websites for trade businesses and make sure they show up on Google when people search for what you do. We also manage your Google Business Profile so you show up in the local map results. Basically: we make it so customers can find you online and actually call you.

---

## "Why should I trust you?"

Fair question. We specialize exclusively in trade businesses — plumbers, electricians, HVAC, roofers, landscapers, contractors. We're month-to-month with no contract, you own your website, and I can show you real examples of other [trade]s we work with in [city].

---

## "Do I own the website?"

Yes, 100%. The domain is registered in your name. All the content, photos, and design are yours. If you ever decide to leave, we help you transition smoothly — no holding your site hostage.

---

## "What if I cancel?"

You give 30 days notice and that's it. No cancellation fee, no penalty. You own the site, so it stays live. The only thing that stops is our monthly SEO and GBP management work. Most clients stay because they see results, not because they're locked in.

---

## "How do I know it's working?"

You get a monthly report showing exactly how many calls and form submissions your site generated, plus where your keywords are ranking on Google. No fluff — real numbers you can verify by checking your own phone.

---

## "Do you do Google Ads?"

We don't. We focus on organic SEO and Google Business Profile — the stuff that brings in leads long-term without paying per click. Some clients run ads on their own alongside our work, and we're happy to coordinate with whoever manages those.

---

## "What about social media?"

Social media is great for brand awareness, but our focus is Google — because that's where people with immediate needs search. Someone looking for a plumber at 7 PM isn't scrolling Instagram. We recommend social as a complement, but Google first.

---

## "How long until I see results?"

SEO takes time — it's not a switch you flip. Most clients see meaningful ranking improvements within 60-90 days. Some local keywords move in weeks, more competitive ones take months. We report honestly on progress every month so you know exactly where things stand.

---

## "Do you need access to my Google account?"

We need access to your Google Business Profile to manage it effectively. We guide you through granting us manager access — you don't share your password, and you retain full ownership. It takes about 2 minutes to set up.

---

## "Will you change my phone number?"

Never. We set up call tracking so we can report how many calls come from the website, but your customers always see and dial YOUR number. The call tracking number forwards to you — you won't even notice it's there.

---

## "Is there a contract?"

No. Month-to-month only. We keep clients because we deliver, not because of a contract. You can leave anytime with 30 days notice.

---

## "What if I already have a website?"

That's fine — we can either build you a new one or optimize the one you have, depending on what makes sense. Most existing trade websites need either a full rebuild or significant SEO work to actually rank. I can take a look and give you an honest assessment for free.

---

## "Can I see examples?"

Absolutely. We have a portfolio of trade websites we've built — plumbers, electricians, roofers, and more. I'll send you links to a couple that are similar to your business after this call. You can see exactly what we deliver.

---

## "Who hosts the website?"

We handle hosting, included in the monthly retainer. Your site runs on fast, secure infrastructure with automatic backups and an SSL certificate. You never have to think about server stuff.

---

## "What happens to my website if I stop paying?"

The site stays live — you own it. But ongoing SEO work, GBP management, hosting, and monthly updates stop. The site won't disappear; it just won't be actively optimized or maintained by us anymore.

---

## "How are you different from other companies?"

Three things: we only work with trade businesses so we actually understand your industry, we're month-to-month with no contract, and we charge $1,100-$1,500 setup plus $300/month — which is a fraction of what generalist agencies charge for the same work.
